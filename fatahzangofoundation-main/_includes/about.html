ABOUT US

The Fatah Zango Foundation, established in 2011 by Dr. Mohammad Musa Zango, is a non-profit, non-governmental organization committed to driving inclusive and sustainable development across Nigeria. With a vision rooted in compassion, equity, and empowerment, the Foundation has impacted thousands of lives through programs that address education, health, entrepreneurship, and community infrastructure. Over the years, we have grown from a local initiative into a nationwide movement, partnering with communities, institutions, and stakeholders to break the cycle of poverty and build capacity at the grassroots level. Our integrated approach combines humanitarian support with long-term development solutions, ensuring individuals are not only supported but also equipped to create lasting change in their own lives and communities. From awarding scholarships to vulnerable students, to training women in trades that lead to economic independence, to organizing life-saving medical outreach and launching micro-enterprise support programs, our work is guided by a firm belief: that every human being deserves the opportunity to thrive with dignity.
